# Contact Us

The Kube-OVN community welcomes all users and developers. You can reach us through the channels below:

## Online Channels

- Slack: join the [CNCF Slack](https://slack.cncf.io) and visit the [#kube-ovn](https://cloud-native.slack.com/archives/C0AQMHUDX39) channel
- LinkedIn: [Kube-OVN official page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/kube-ovn/)
- Issues / Discussions: report issues on [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/kubeovn/kube-ovn/issues), or join design discussions on [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/kubeovn/kube-ovn/discussions)
